The more specific you are, the faster I can give you a quote:

1. **What task** are you doing manually that you want automated?
2. **How often** do you do it? (daily, weekly, per-customer?)
3. **What tools** are you using? (Gmail, Slack, Airtable, etc.)
4. **What's the cost** of doing it manually? (time, money, errors?)
